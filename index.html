Notation Guide: So uhh if you don't know, here is my explanation. E100#2#3#2 (hyper-e notation) represents E100^E100 which is the first step (E100#2). Then after you get the result you do result^result^result (E100#2#3) and then that result^that result.

If E100#0(#1^5) it just means E100#1#1#1#1#1
